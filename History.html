Our website was inspired by our senior project

Our senior project focuses on developing an intelligent web-based tool that utilizes gene expression data to predict the type of cancer in patients. The project aims to address the most common cancer types in Saudi Arabia, including breast, colorectal, thyroid, liver, and kidney cancers. By analyzing gene expression profiles, the tool will assist oncologists in diagnosing cancer.
